Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Description

This Creamy Mushroom Pasta speaks for itself. A slippery tangle of pasta bathed in a creamy parmesan sauce loaded with buttery garlic mushrooms, it's simple, quick, and utterly luxurious!

Ingredients

  • Mushrom
  • White Wine
  • Parmesan
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water for 1 minute less than the package time (start this while cooking mushrooms). Before draining, scoop out 1 cup of cooking water, then drain the pasta.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter and heat oil over high heat.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook, stirring regularly. When they start to sweat, add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. When mushrooms are golden (about 4-5 minutes), add garlic and cook until both are golden.
  5. Stand back and add wine (it will be steamy!). Stir and scrape the bottom of the skillet. Simmer until the wine is mostly evaporated and the smell is gone.
  6. Add broth, cream, parmesan, salt, and pepper. Stir to dissolve the parmesan and simmer for 2 minutes, stirring regularly.
  7. Transfer the drained pasta into the sauce and toss for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the pasta. If the sauce gets too thick, add a splash of reserved pasta cooking water.
  8. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately with extra parmesan.
